# MVP Strategy: Find Your Campus

## Core Thesis
Students are making high-stakes admission decisions based on biased marketing. Connecting them with current seniors provides the ground truth they need.

## Validation Metrics
1. **Lead Conversion**: % of visitors who fill the interest form.
2. **Willingness to Pay**: % of leads who proceed to the ₹99 payment.
3. **Engagement**: Number of successful WhatsApp connections.

## Manual Onboarding (The "Do Things That Don't Scale" Phase)
- We use a simple UPI QR code for payments to avoid PG integration overhead.
- Onboarding is handled manually via WhatsApp.
- Mentors are contacted manually for the first 10-20 sessions to refine the process.
